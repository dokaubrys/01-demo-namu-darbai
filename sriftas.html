Delving through the vibrant underground party scene of Jakarta, Indonesia. With curiousity in the sounds of percussions, organic compositions, analog synthesizers and drum machines helped to shape a keen fascination towards music comprising these elements. He has had the opportunity to share parties with the finest international DJs such as Bell Towers, Thomas Bullock, Fumihiko Sato Neil Diablo, Eric Duncan, San Soda, Horse Meat Disco, Kenji Takimi, Dicky Trisco, Komodo, and Jonathan Kusuma,. A member of a musical project known as Sunmantra with recent releases on Nein and Roam records. His Most recent release the 12” EP with berlin’s Soundmetaphors and an edit track on Kusuma’s Label Dekadenz Records.
